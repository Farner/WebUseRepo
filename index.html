Our Vision

Here, at Laurent Saison, we believe that the quiet elegance of the mountains belongs to everyone— not just those with private chalets, monogrammed luggage, or a family tree older than the Matterhorn. We’re here to make old-money charm feel less like an exclusive club, and more like a feeling you can step into—calm, confident, and beautifully put-together.

To us, great style isn’t about wealth. It’s about intention. It’s about choosing pieces that make you feel grounded, capable, and subtly refined, whether you’re walking into a café or watching the first snowfall settle on the peaks.

Our inspiration comes from nature: the clean lines of fresh snow, the warmth of wood and wool, the timeless dignity of alpine living.

We want to bring that feeling to everyone— no trust fund required. Just a love for the outdoors, an appreciation for craftsmanship, and the desire to look effortlessly elegant.

Our Classic Jean Project

We craft jeans with intention. Clean lines. Quiet confidence. Pieces that feel effortless— yet unmistakably refined.

Inspired by the stillness of the mountains and the dignity of timeless style, our jeans are made to elevate the everyday.

Not loud. Not indulgent. Just honest craftsmanship. Natural textures. A silhouette that respects the body and the landscape.

We design for people who value simplicity. For those who want to feel composed, comfortable, and quietly elegant— without needing wealth to express it.

This is our vision, woven into every thread: Luxury that feels grounded. Style that feels natural. Clothing that lets you breathe.

The beginning of something timeless.

Om oss

Les Deux Hommes

Our logo tells the story of where Laurent Saison began.

Two figures on skis —
two friends carving their way down the slopes of our Norwegian homeland —
captured in a moment of pure winter simplicity.
Not chasing speed.
Not chasing perfection.
Only the quiet desire to look composed, confident, and well-put-together
even in the coldest wind.

It started as a personal detail:
the way we carried ourselves on the mountain,
how style could elevate an ordinary day on the slopes,
and how a shared love for clean lines, wool layers, and alpine stillness
became something we wanted to bring to others.

That moment became our mark.
A symbol of friendship, craftsmanship, and the belief that elegance doesn’t belong only to those with private chalets or old family crests.
It belongs to anyone who appreciates the dignity of timeless design.

Our vision flows through the logo itself:
that the quiet elegance of the mountains should be accessible to all —
not exclusive, but inviting.
That great style isn’t about wealth,
but about intention.
About feeling grounded, capable, and subtly refined.
About finding beauty in restraint, nature, and the way good clothing lets you move freely.

Two men on skis.
A simple silhouette.
A reminder of where we come from — and of what we stand for.

Laurent Saison:
where style feels natural,
where luxury feels honest,
and where the mountains — whether Norwegian or Swiss — always feel like home.
